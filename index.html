About Roberto

I was born and raised in Buenos Aires, Argentina, where I developed myself as a musician both recording and producing.  My passion for the audio/visual arts has taken me all over the globe, and the journey hasn’t even started.  I'm a firm believer on creating honest and genuine, quality content.  I have a background in video and music production together with social media strategy and analytics.  Today, I live in Austin, TX, where I'm finishing my degree on Digital Media Management.  When I finish, I'm  moving to Los Angeles, where the entertainment industry thrives and dies and pacific waves crash the shores every day.  I'm an ambassador of rock n' roll, ready to leave my mark on the world.
